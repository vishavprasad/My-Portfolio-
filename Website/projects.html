Extra Curricular Activities

IQ Active 2024

I had the opportunity to participate in the Fiji IQ Active Competition, a national academic quiz organized by the Ministry of Education and broadcasted on Fiji TV. The competition brings together students from various secondary schools across the country, including those from remote areas, to test their general knowledge, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Taking part in this competition was both challenging and rewarding, as it pushed me to think quickly, work collaboratively in a team, and expand my understanding of current events and academic concepts beyond the classroom. It was a great platform to build confidence and develop strong communication and analytical skills in a competitive yet encouraging environment.

Clean-Up Campaign 2024

I also took part in the Kinoya Clean-Up Campaign, an environmental initiative organized by our school to promote community awareness and responsibility. The campaign involved students actively cleaning public spaces in and around the Kinoya area, including roadsides, drains, and common areas affected by litter. It was a hands-on experience that taught me the importance of environmental sustainability, teamwork, and civic duty. Through this initiative, I developed a deeper sense of respect for my community and understood the real impact small actions can have on creating a cleaner and healthier environment. Being part of the campaign was both fulfilling and eye-opening, and it inspired me to continue supporting community-based efforts in the future.

Clubs

As a member of the school's Landscaping Club, I actively take part in clearing land and planting flowers around the school environment. This hands-on work not only helps beautify the school grounds but also provides an opportunity to grow and care for plants that can later be sold to teachers and students. Through this activity, I am learning valuable skills in gardening, teamwork, and small-scale entrepreneurship.
